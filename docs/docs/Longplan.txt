3. **Microservices Architecture**
   - Independent scaling of services
   - Fault isolation
   - Easy feature deployment

## ⚠️ **RISK MITIGATION**

### Technical Risks
1. **AI Model Accuracy**
   - **Risk**: Inconsistent grading results
   - **Mitigation**: Continuous model training, human validation loops

2. **Scalability Issues**
   - **Risk**: System overload during peak usage
   - **Mitigation**: Auto-scaling infrastructure, load testing

3. **Data Security**
   - **Risk**: Customer data breaches
   - **Mitigation**: End-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance

### Business Risks
1. **Market Acceptance**
   - **Risk**: Users prefer traditional grading
   - **Mitigation**: Position as complementary tool initially

2. **Competitive Response**
   - **Risk**: Major graders develop competing AI
   - **Mitigation**: Strong IP portfolio, feature velocity

3. **Regulatory Changes**
   - **Risk**: Collectibles industry regulation
   - **Mitigation**: Compliance monitoring, legal partnerships

### Financial Risks
1. **Customer Acquisition Cost**
